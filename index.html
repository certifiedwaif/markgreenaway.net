I'm Mark Greenaway, a computational statistician and software engineer.

Computational Statistics and Data Science

I did my PhD under A/Prof. John Ormerod at the University of Sydney. I did my research on variational approximations to zero-inflated Poisson mixed models and model selection for linear models using g-priors. Here's the GitHub repository for my blma package, written in C++ using the Eigen and GSL libraries and parallelised using OpenMP.

I also have a strong interest in computational linear algebra and numerical methods/numerical analysis. I'm an enormous fan of Professor Gilbert Strang at MIT, and think his 18.06 and 18.065 courses are the best way to learn or relearn linear algebra.
